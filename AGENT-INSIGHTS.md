#### Medium Priority

5. **Add "As Featured In" badges**

   - Rationale: Third-party validation is powerful

6. **Track social media referrals**

- **Your biggest asset is your story**, not your follower count. "Banned from 4 platforms for being too effective" is a hook that sells.
